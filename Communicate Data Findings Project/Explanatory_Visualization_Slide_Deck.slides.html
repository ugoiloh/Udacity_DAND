Fordgo Bike Services, San Francisco Bay Area¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I'm interested in observing the effects of some elements associated with bike services. I would be analysing time, user behavior, bike types, to see how they impart the business.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted a recent Fordgo bike trips data. It's for May 2020 and has approximately 138,000 trip records. The attributes include time of start and end of trips, from which I calculated the duration of each trip, week day of each trip, period of the day, hour of start for each trip. These engineered data were the basics of my analysis. The data can be accessed here

Hourly Distribution of trips¶

As expected, less number of trips occurred during the night hours as people would naturally prefer to sleep in the night. On the other hand, the majority of trips occurred during the day, with 18:00 hrs, having the most trips. This could be due to cool weather associated with evening periods of the day. Overall, the distribution is approximately normal.

User type vs bike type for Trips duration less than 28 mins¶

It was observed during the data wrangling progress that 75% of trip duration is below 28 minutes. So, I trimmed the data to capture only that range to have clear distribution of the data.

As indicated in the visual, the trip durations for subscribed users (members) are less compared to the casual users. There is also more trips taken by members with lesser durations as indicated by the shape of the plot.

This suggest that members use bikes more for official purposes.

Summary¶

  • The Fordgo company received the highest patronage from casual users in May 2020
  • The preference for electric bikes is high among users.
  • Electric bikes are faster.
  • Subscribed users (members) generally ride short distances
  • The casual users generally use bike services more for outdoor activities, hence they spend more time on rides.
  • More rides are doneon weekends.
  • Users ride more often during evening hours.
  • Trips started around 12am and 4-5am takes more.
